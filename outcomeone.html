1.7. General Data Protection Registration

+Legal

Resgistration shows that a business is legal and compliant, or simply trust worthy.
It forces the company to be compliant to all of the current requirements for safe business practice avoiding legal action from anyone.

+Legal Action

The registration allows the company to avoid fines by following the 8 data protection principles that are enforced.
Fines can be as large as $700,000.

+Customer Security

Once registered the company will have a data protection number which can be used to improve networking.
Storage of data and information the company with holds will be dealt with well under the regulations.


-Strict Maintenance

As a data controller the data and information withheld must be used within the GDPR regulations.

-Cost

The cost is inconsiderate in comparison to the consiquences of not abiding or using the GDPR.

-Training

Staff must all be trained to properly hand data and information within the company.
The training must cover all of the GDPR regulations.

-Procedures

Encryption and analysis of where people are working for example must be considered, as the practice must be law abiding.
