  • +Fines

    • The registration allows the company to avoid fines by following the 8 data protection principles that forces compliance.
    Fines can be as large as $700,000.

  • +Customer Security

    • Once registered the company will have a data protection number which can be used to improve networking.
    Storage of data and information the company with holds will be dealt with well under the regulations.
